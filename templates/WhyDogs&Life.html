Why Dogs&life ?


Who we are.

We are just like you, someone who cares enough to make a difference by getting involved. We are a non-profit, 100% volunteer run organization that operates on the strength of our dedicated volunteers that have an overwhelming sense of compassion and love for our canine companions.

Through our active network of dedicated volunteers and fosters, we are able to make a real difference in the lives of thousands of dogs from shelters in Bangalore and across Karnataka.


Why we exist.

To provide a safe haven and the care needed for adoptable large breed dogs and puppies that would otherwise die in shelters or on the streets.
To educate the public on what it takes to own a dog and the care they need to live healthy rewarding lives.

To give neglected and abused animals a new chance to see the kindness in humans. We work to bring families and dogs together for a lifelong journey of love and respect.



What we believe.

Our friends we call dogs deserve a dignified existence regardless of age, health, breed or where they came from. Dogs are resilient creatures who, with love, security, proper nutrition and medical care can most often be reintegrated into a home to bond with a forever family.
