How can you donate?


YOUR DONATIONS SAVE LIVES! Dogs&Life is a 100% volunteer-run, non-profit organization.
We are a group of individuals dedicating our time, money, homes, cars, and hearts to saving
and rescuing dogs.

Most may not know this, but the average adoption donation covers less than half the expense
to save and care for each dog that is saved. All donations go directly to care for the dogs we save.

This care includes vaccinations, spay and neuter, life-saving surgeries, and emergency treatment
for rescued dogs with life-threatening injuries. Your donation will go a long way in helping us give
rescued dogs a second chance at life and match them with a loving family.

Thank you for supporting the work that helps thousands of dogs across Karnataka each year.
