STEP 1: Decide what kind of dog you want.

Yes! There are many kinds of dogs you may come across, each varying in their behaviour,character,size,etc.

Keep in mind your lifestyle, budget, home space and your family before you decide which kind of dog you want to bring home. While families with kids or young couples might opt for a lively dog, seniors can go for aged canines who require less training and can be ideal retirement companions.



STEP 2: Find out your near by shelters.

Locate the nearby dog shelter and visit the place several times keeping in mind who you want in your home. Dogs&Life is a center situated in bangalore.


STEP 3: Bond with the dogs at the center.

Interact with the dogs at the shelter.

Ask the shelter keepers about their individual temperament and history.
Enquire about their behaviour with humans and other animals.

Ideally, visit the shelter a couple of times and bond with your ‘chosen one’ before
adopting her/him. Make an informed decision in the end.


STEP 4: Secure the medical records.

Ensure that your dog is properly vaccinated. Ask the shelter to provide the medical records of the dog, detailing past illnesses or injuries/traumas, if any.


STEP 5: Follow the official adoption procedure.
Most shelters follow a specific adoption procedure involving an adoption form and submission of other certificates.

So do we! The adoption form asks questions about your original identity documents and address proof as you will be obliged to provide a copy to the center for saftey reasons.


STEP 6: Take your fur baby home.

Finally the moment you've been waiting for is here.

After requisite background checks and counselling, the shelter will finally hand over the dog to you.

However, it might take the dog some time to acclimatise to the home environment. For some, it might be just a few days, while other dogs can take up to a few months.
